A four-year respiratory crisis spread through factory cities as coal smoke, metal dust and Resonant contamination combined.

## What happened

Doctors disagreed whether the illness was chemical, infectious or occult; workers named it for the brass valves fitted to the worst sufferers.

## Outcome

Respirators and municipal air sirens became ordinary features of urban life.
