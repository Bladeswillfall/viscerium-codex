A proposal to recognise selected Myrkild intelligences as legal persons divided human governments and hunter orders.

## What happened

Supporters argued that some Myrkild had lived beside humanity for centuries; opponents called personhood another predatory disguise.

## Outcome

No universal amnesty passed, but local recognition regimes emerged.
