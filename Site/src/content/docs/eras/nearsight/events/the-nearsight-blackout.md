A coordinated systems failure blinded military, civic and orbital sensors across multiple continents.

## What happened

Screens glowed and scanners returned numbers, but independent systems no longer agreed on position, time or identity.

## Outcome

Civilisation retained machines but lost confidence that its instruments described the same world.
