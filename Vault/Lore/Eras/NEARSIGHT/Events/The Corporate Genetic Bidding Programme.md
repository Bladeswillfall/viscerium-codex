# The Corporate Genetic Bidding Programme

States auctioned exclusive enhancement contracts to corporations in exchange for population-scale genetic services.

## What happened

Public schedules promised disease resistance; sealed schedules priced aggression, fertility, cognition and military compatibility.

## Outcome

Genetic traits became strategic assets allocated through corporate concessions.
