# The RD-76 Dart Plunge Trial

An RD-76 Dart completed a controlled near-orbital nose-down plunge and recovered under its own power.

## What happened

The craft crossed the sound barrier nose-first, shed heat through sacrificial surfaces and transitioned to powered atmospheric flight.

## Outcome

Rapid orbital insertion became credible for cargo, troops and strategic recovery.
