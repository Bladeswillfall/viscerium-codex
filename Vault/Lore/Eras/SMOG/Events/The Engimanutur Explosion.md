# The Engimanutur Explosion

A leap-day pressure failure destroyed the experimental lower furnaces beneath Harkworks and opened a persistent Resonant scar.

## What happened

Boilers ruptured in sequence, but investigators found several pressure vessels crushed inward rather than burst outward.

## Outcome

The lower works were sealed and the surrounding district remained uninhabitable.
