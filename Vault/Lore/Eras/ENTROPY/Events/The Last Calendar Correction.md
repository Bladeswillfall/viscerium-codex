# The Last Calendar Correction

On a leap intercalary day, the major human polities reconciled their drifting clocks to one civil chronology for the last time.

## What happened

Relativistic travel, gate transit and altered memory had produced calendars that disagreed on which events had occurred.

## Outcome

A final shared date was established even though no authority could enforce future synchronisation.
