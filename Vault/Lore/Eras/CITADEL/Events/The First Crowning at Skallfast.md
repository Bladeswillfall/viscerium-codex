# The First Crowning at Skallfast

The oath-crowning that transformed a ring of fortified valleys into the first recognised Okse Dominion.

## What happened

Seven rulers entered Skallfast as peers. Six left as sworn shields beneath Hroth Varn, while the seventh was carried out beneath a woollen shroud.

## Outcome

The valley kings accepted one war-crown and a common levy.
