# The Galdyr Compact

A month-long conclave bound rival smith-priests to shared measures for powder, steel and Resonant workmanship.

## What happened

The compact ensured that a barrel, bolt or ward-stone purchased in one stronghold could be repaired in another.

## Outcome

Weapon and tool standards spread between otherwise hostile citadels.
