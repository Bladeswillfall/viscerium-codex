# The Silence After Seven Bells

On the morning after the bells rang, every child born within Karn Vey was silent until sunset.

## What happened

Later copies disagree on the number of births and whether the silence lasted until sunset or only until the bells were dismantled.

## Outcome

The event was omitted from civic records but preserved in midwives' rolls.
