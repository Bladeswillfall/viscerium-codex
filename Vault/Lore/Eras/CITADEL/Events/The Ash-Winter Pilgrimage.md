# The Ash-Winter Pilgrimage

A disputed three-year migration in which Resonant penitents crossed the burned north and returned speaking in borrowed voices.

## What happened

No surviving register agrees on how many pilgrims departed, which road they took, or whether the people who returned were the same people.

## Outcome

New Resonant cults spread through the northern keeps.
