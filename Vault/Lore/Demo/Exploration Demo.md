> **NON-CANON / DISPOSABLE TEST CONTENT.** None of the names, places, factions, dates, or relationships on these demo pages belong to VISCERIUM canon.

## Atlas checklist

1. Open **Exploration Demo World** from the Atlas index.
2. Search for `Demo Gate City`, `Demo Frontier Fort`, `Demo Trade Port`, and `Demo Rift Site`.
3. Toggle the Civilisation, Military, Infrastructure, Political, and Threat layer branches.
4. Zoom in to reveal **Demo Rift Site** and then **Demo Hidden Ruin** at progressively closer levels.
5. Open **Demo Gate City** from its marker and confirm the nested city map loads.
6. On the city map, search for **Demo Market Ward** and zoom in to reveal **Demo Archive Tower**.

## Relationships checklist

1. Open **Relationships** under Explore.
2. Search for **Demo Concord**.
3. Filter between `Allies`, `Rivals`, `Member Of`, and `Commands`.
4. Select the alliance/rival edges and confirm their metadata appears.
5. Switch to hierarchy layout and inspect **Demo Marshal → Demo Frontier Fort** and **Demo Marshal → Demo Concord**.
6. Confirm the reciprocal **Demo Concord ↔ Demo League** alliance is represented once rather than duplicated.

## Removal

Delete `Vault/Lore/Demo/` and the two `exploration-demo-*.svg` files from `Vault/Assets/Maps/` once the exploration features have been visually accepted.
